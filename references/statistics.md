# AI Search Visibility: Key Statistics

Use these in audits, proposals, and client-facing materials. All have attribution.

## AI Search Shift
- 25% of organic search traffic shifting to AI chatbots by end of 2026 (Gartner)
- 64% of global searches result in zero clicks (Ahrefs, mid-2025)
- 58.5% of Google searches end without a click (BrightEdge, 2025)
- AI Overviews appear in 50%+ of all Google searches (White Peak, Aug 2025)
- 47% of AI Overview citations come from pages ranking below position 5

## AI Citations and Content
- Reddit is the #1 AI citation source: 40.1% of all AI citations (Profound, 2025)
- Answer capsules increase AI citations by 40% (Attainment internal testing)
- Proper schema increases visibility by 30-40%
- Original data increases AI citations by 30-40%
- Author credentials increase AI citations by 40%
- Multi-modal content (text + images + schema) sees up to 317% higher AI citation rate
- Content updated within 30 days earns 3.2x more AI Overview citations
- 82.5% of AI Overview citations go to "deep" content pages, only 0.5% to homepages
- Tables get cited 2.5x more than plain text in the same information format
- 44% of ChatGPT citations come from the first third of the content

## AI Brand Accuracy
- 33-48% of AI responses contain factual errors about brands (PersonQA benchmark)
- 58% of consumers lose trust after seeing AI misrepresent a brand (Edelman, 2025)
- Brands on 4+ platforms are 2.8x more likely to appear in ChatGPT responses
- Only 11-12% of cited domains overlap between ChatGPT and Perplexity

## Search Behavior
- 64% of Gen Z use TikTok as a search engine (Google internal research)
- 51% of Gen Z prefer TikTok over Google for search
- 8+ word queries are 7x more likely to trigger Google AI Overviews
- GEO search interest surged 121% Q3/Q4 2025 (Search Engine Land)
- 80M weekly Reddit searches (Reddit, 2025)

## Voice and Visual
- 50%+ of online searches are voice searches (2025)
- 75% of US households own a smart speaker
- 12 billion+ visual searches per month on Google Lens (Google, 2025)
- $62 billion global voice shopping projected (2025)

## Agentic Commerce
- Agentic traffic increased 1,300% Jan-Aug 2025 (BrightEdge)
- Shopify AI orders grew 11x since Jan 2025
- 47% of US shoppers use AI for at least one shopping task (2025)
- $300-500B US agentic commerce by 2030 (Bain)
- $3-5T global agentic commerce by 2030 (McKinsey)
- 900,000+ Amazon sellers using AI listing tools (2025)

## Page Speed and AI Citations
- FCP under 0.4s: 6.7 AI citations average (SE Ranking)
- FCP over 1.13s: 2.1 AI citations average
- Page speed affects AI citation rate because slow pages increase crawl abandonment by AI bots
